Anti-Virus Guidelines

Last Update Status: June 2021

Recommended processes to prevent virus problems on your laptop or computer:

  • Always run supported and actively updated anti-virus software.
  • Always download and install anti-virus software updates as they become available.
  • NEVER open any files or macros attached to an email from an unknown, suspicious or untrustworthy source. Delete these attachments immediately, then "double delete" them by emptying your Trash/Deleted Items.
  • Delete spam, chain, and other junk email without forwarding, in accordance with The Email Acceptable Use Policy and The Automatically Forwarded Email Policy.
  • Never download files from unknown or suspicious sources.
  • Avoid direct disk sharing with read/write access unless there is a crucial business requirement to do so.
  • Always scan a removable media (floppy diskettes, CDs, USBs, etc) from an unknown source for viruses before using it.
  • Back-up critical data and system configurations on a regular basis and store the data in a safe place in accordance with The Information Backups Policy.
