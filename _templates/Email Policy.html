Email Policy

Last Update Status: June 2021

1. Overview

Electronic email is widely used in almost all industries and is often the primary communication and awareness method within an organization. At the same time, misuse of email can post many legal, privacy and security risks, thus it’s important for users to understand the appropriate use of electronic communications.

2. Purpose

The purpose of this email policy is to ensure the proper use of Property.CoZa’s email system and make users aware of what Property.CoZa deems as acceptable and unacceptable use of its email system. This policy outlines the minimum requirements for use of email within the Property.CoZa Network.

3. Scope

This policy covers appropriate use of any email sent from a Property.CoZa email address and applies to all employees, vendors, contractors, consultants, and agents operating on behalf of Property.CoZa including all usersaffiliated with third parties

4. Policy

2.a All use of email must be consistent with Property.CoZa policies and procedures of ethical conduct, safety, compliance with applicable laws and proper business practices.

2.b Property.CoZa email accounts should be used primarily for Property.CoZa business-related purposes, personal communication is permitted on a limited basis, but non-Property.CoZa related commercial uses are prohibited.

2.c All Property.CoZa data contained within an email message or an attachment must be secured according to the Data Protection Standard.

2.d Email should be retained only if it qualifies as a Property.CoZa business record. Email is a Property.CoZa business record if there exists a legitimate and ongoing business reason to preserve the information contained in the email.

2.e Email that is identified as a Property.CoZa business record shall be retained according to Property.CoZa Record Retention Schedule.

2.f The Property.CoZa email system shall not be used for the creation or distribution of any disruptive or offensive messages, including offensive comments about race, gender, hair color, disabilities, age, sexual orientation, pornography, religious beliefs and practice, political beliefs, or national origin. Employees who receive any emails with this content from any Property.CoZa employee should report the matter to their supervisor immediately.

2.g Users are prohibited from automatically forwarding Property.CoZa email to a third party email system (noted in 4.8 below). Individual messages which are forwarded by the user must not contain Property.CoZa confidential or above information. Automatically forwarded email must adhere to the Automatically Forwarded Email Policy.

2.h Users are prohibited from using third-party email systems and storage servers such as Google, Yahoo, and MSN Hotmail etc. to conduct Property.CoZa business, to create or honour any binding transactions, or to store or retain email on behalf of Property.CoZa.Such communications and transactions should be conducted through proper channels using Property.CoZa-approved documentation.

2.i Using a reasonable amount of Property.CoZa resources for personal emails is acceptable, but non-work related email shall be saved in a separate folder from work related email. Sending chain letters or joke emails from a Property.CoZa email account is prohibited.

2.j Property.CoZa employees shall have no expectation of privacy in anything they store, send or receive on the company’s email system.

2.k Property.CoZa may monitor messages without prior notice.

2.l Property.CoZa is not obliged to monitor email messages.

5. Policy Compliance

(5.1) Compliance Measurement

Property.CoZa will verify compliance to this policy through various methods, including but not limited to, periodic walk-throughs, video monitoring, business tool reports, internal and external audits, and feedback to the policy owner.

(5.2) Exceptions

Any exception to the policy must be approved by Property.CoZa in advance.

(5.3) Non-Compliance

Any person(s) found to have violated this policy may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment/Independent Contractor Agreement.
